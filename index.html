Step 1
すみません
The magic word
Step 1 · The magic word
すみません su · mi · ma · sen
Excuse me
Calling someone's attention
Sorry
Apologising to someone
Pardon me
Passing in front of someone
Situation ①
レストランで — At this restaurant
The food looks amazing. You've been waiting 10 minutes. The waiter hasn't noticed you.
What do you say?
なんて いいますか？
Situation ① — Answer
Calling staff in a restaurant
すみません！ sumimasen!
Raise your hand as you say it — staff will come right away.
Situation ②
まちで — On a busy street like this
You're walking through Harajuku. You accidentally bump into someone.
What do you say?
なんて いいますか？
Situation ② — Answer
Saying sorry — bumped into someone
すみません！ sumimasen!
A quick bow adds even more politeness. Japanese people will appreciate it.
Situation ③
とおりすぎる — Passing by
Someone is standing in front of you. You need to pass in front of them.
What do you say?
なんて いいますか？
Situation ③ — Answer
Passing in front of someone
すみません sumimasen
A small bow as you pass. No need to stop — just acknowledge them.
Now say it together — 3 times
すみません
すみません
すみません
sumimasen · sumimasen · sumimasen
Step 2
みちを きく
Asking the way
Step 2 · Master this one pattern
〜は どこですか？ ~ wa doko desu ka? "Where is ~ ?"
Replace 〜 with any place. That's all you need to ask directions anywhere in Japan.
Step 2 · Word to know
えき eki — station
えき は どこですか？ eki wa doko desu ka? — "Where is the station?"
Step 2 · Another essential word
トイレ toire — restroom
トイレ は どこですか？ toire wa doko desu ka? — "Where is the restroom?"
Real Japan Tip
Ask for help —
they'll walk you there
Japanese people are known for going out of their way to help. Ask once, and they may walk you all the way to your destination.
Don't be shy — just say すみません
Step 2 · After they help you
ありがとう arigatou Thank you
ついて いって、さいごに ひとこと — follow them, then say this at the end.

Step 3
ともだちを
つくる
Make a friend
Step 3 · Ask their name
なまえは
なんですか？ namae wa nan desu ka? "What's your name?"
あいての なまえを きいて みよう — ask the person next to you right now!
Step 3 · Answer like this
___ です。 ___ desu. — "I'm ___."
Your name + です。That's it. The simplest way to introduce yourself in Japanese.
Step 3 · Full dialogue
A
なまえは なんですか？
namae wa nan desu ka? — "What's your name?"
B
ジョン です。
John desu. — "I'm John."
Both
よろしく おねがいします！
yoroshiku onegai shimasu! — "Nice to meet you!"
